# Rename-Global-Warming
Team work repository.
Team members: Kostiantyn (@kostiantynkovalchuk) Ly-ly (@lylyjan) Said (@FrenchFry36)

The 2024 DANA (Depresión Aislada en Niveles Altos) caused unprecedented devastation in Valencia region. 
In a matter of hours, rainfall surpassed 500 liters per square meter in several areas, overwhelming rivers like the Magre and Xúquer, which led to widespread flooding and destruction of infrastructure. This storm caused at least 92 deaths, displaced thousands, and left hundreds missing, with entire neighborhoods submerged and thousands of vehicles stranded.

We build a website letting people come up with a better and more suitable name for such a serious threat. 
We raise awareness. We raise funds to support a GW research/ help people.

We want to offer Rename Global Warming project’s database to a European government agencies and organizations focused on climate policy, environmental research, and sustainability:

1.⁠ ⁠European Environment Agency (EEA)
	•	Website: https://www.eea.europa.eu
	•	Why? The EEA collects and analyzes environmental data across Europe to support climate policies. Your database could contribute to their research and reports.

2.⁠ ⁠European Commission – Directorate-General for Climate Action (DG CLIMA)
	•	Website: https://climate.ec.europa.eu
	•	Why? This department develops and implements EU climate policies, including the European Green Deal and carbon neutrality goals. Your project could support public awareness initiatives.

3.⁠ ⁠European Parliament – Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Food Safety (ENVI)
	•	Website: https://www.europarl.europa.eu/committees/en/envi/home.html
	•	Why? This committee shapes EU climate laws and could use your database to understand public sentiment and communication strategies around global warming.

4.⁠ ⁠Joint Research Centre (JRC) of the European Commission
	•	Website: https://joint-research-centre.ec.europa.eu
	•	Why? The JRC provides independent scientific research for EU policies, including climate change. Your data could enhance their studies on public perception of environmental issues.

5.⁠ ⁠European Climate Pact (Under the European Green Deal)
	•	Website: https://climate-pact.europa.eu
	•	Why? This initiative encourages citizen and organizational involvement in climate action. Your project could align with their goal of improving climate communication.
